## What this means

Forecasts are coherent across every planning level — store totals sum to category totals, which sum to the grand total — so store managers, category buyers, and finance plan against the same numbers. The error-cost figure prices the forecast's mistakes asymmetrically: a stockout costs the full margin lost, an overstock costs the capital tied up. That is the number to drive down, not raw accuracy alone.
